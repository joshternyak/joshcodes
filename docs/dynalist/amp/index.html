Dynalist: Simple Todo List & Note-Taking App For Productivity

by Josh Ternyak

2020-05-06

I started using Dynalist in the summer of 2019 and my life has changed ever since. Dynalist is a simple todo list and note taking app that helps you stay organized and focused on what's important. Here are 8 reasons why Dynalist is one of the best todo list apps ever made and why you should use it everyday.

Dynalist Is Very Easy To Use

I use Dynalist everyday to keep track of my daily and weekly tasks. It helps me be the best web developer and student I can be. It organizes my life, business, and brain. When I say it's easy to use, I mean it. You can easily create and check off tasks in bulleted lists. Compared to other todo lists, Dynalist is the easiest to use.

As shown in the image below, you can organize your weeks, days, and even years. Personally, I have my Dynalist sidebar organized in 4 folders (school, business, goals/plans, and personal). You can have your Dynalist structured anyway you like.

It Keeps You Organized

Not only is Dynalist very easy to use, it also keeps you organized and focused. Instead of trying to remember everything you have to do today, tomorrow, and next week, simply make tasks in Dynalist in seconds.

Ever since I started keeping my tasks in Dynalist, I've been more organized with a clear and focused mind.

Your life will significantly improve once you start using this awesome todo list.

You Can Plan Your Life

Planning what you want to do and accomplish in life is critical to your success. I have made many accomplishments ever since I started using Dynalist. It really does help to have a simple and easy to use todo list available right at your finger tips.

As Benjamin Franklin once said:

"By failing to prepare, you are preparing to fail."

He was absolutely correct. When you don't prepare and plan your life, you're preparing to be unorganized and ready to go wherever life takes you. Instead, you must control your life, what you do, and what you accomplish.

Dynalist Is Completely Customizable

You can add dates and filter by date and time, convert any list to a checklist, sync dates in Dynalist to your Google Calendar, and more. You can easily navigate to any item, manipulate multiple items at once, bookmark anything for quick access, group, and filter items.

If you want to share your tasks with others, you can work together with flexible permissions and collaboration and upload files up to 50 MB in size. This is just a fraction of what you can do with Dynalist. To learn more, click here to see more features.

You Will Be More Productive

Using Dynalist will make you more productive, get more done, and focus for longer periods of time. You'll know everything you have to do today, tomorrow, and even the following week.

Getting lots of things done is something necessary to having a productive life, and with Dynalist, you'll get more things done without the stress of having to remember what must be done.  

You Can Organize Your Life

Using Dynalist, I plan my life years ahead. This gives me a peaceful mind knowing what I will accomplish in the future. It helps me plan my projects, school work, and miscellaneous tasks like doing chores.

It's Great For Students

If you're in middle school, high school, or college, Dynalist will help you keep track of your assignments, projects, reports, and everything in between.

You will save yourself from future headaches by planning when assignments or projects are due, and how long you have to complete them.

You can also use Dynalist for taking notes which will help you get better grades in your classes. As a student myself, I can tell you that Dynalist is the best note taking app you'll find in 2020.

Working From Home Is Easy With Dynalist

When you work from home, you have many distractions around you. Whether it's your TV, or even that funny youtube video you were watching earlier. Dynalist helps you stay productive while working from home.

It keeps track of any tasks you need to do today, and tasks that you might be procrastinating on. Working from home is easy if you have a structured daily routine.

Well, Dynalist also helps you make a structured daily routine. Here's my daily routine. It consists of a morning routine, school, business, and ending the day by reading a book. I go more into detail about having a structured daily routine in my productivity guide.

Wrapping Things Up

To conclude, Dynalist is very easy to use, keeps you organized, helps you plan your life, is completely customizable, helps you be more productive, and is great for students. I highly recommend signing up today, it's free.
