The Josh Ternyak Podcast

The official podcast for Josh Ternyak, a web developer enthusiast who shares his experience and knowledge with young entrepreneurs, web developers, and tech geeks around the entire world.

Here are all of the episodes ever published on the Josh Ternyak Podcast.

Asher Shertok - Building, Learning, & Productivity - Episode #6

Asher Shertok is a computer wizard that has built his own computer, security system, electric generator, and even a rope swing. We talk about building, learning, creativity, and finding your passion.

[COPYWRITING TIPS] Copywriting: What I Learned From Reviewing Amazon Products - Episode #5

Copywriting is a skill that many young entrepreneurs want to master. There are all kinds of ways to make money with copywriting. I spent the last 3 months learning copywriting, and writing in-depth reviews of the best vacuums, stain removers, and everything in between.

[WEB DEVELOPER LESSONS] 20 Lessons I've Learned As A 15 Year Old Web Developer - Episode #4

Being a web developer is difficult, stressful, confusing, and exhausting. But, it's also very exciting, interesting, and meaningful. I've learned many lessons during my journey of becoming a web developer at a young age.

Here are 20 lessons I learned that will help you be a better web developer and more successful in life in general.

[BE MORE PRODUCTIVE] Productivity: Time & Task Management Strategies That Work - Episode #3

This is the ultimate guide to being productive, day-in and day-out, getting more done, accomplishing your goals, staying organized, and more.

Being productive is something all of us want. It's very easy to do unproductive things, everyday. However, if you have a time and task management system that you use and implement daily, you will master the skill of productivity.

[LEARN WEB DEVELOPMENT] The Ultimate Web Development Crash Course - Episode #2

This episode is about everything I've learned in my web developer journey. It will help you understand how to learn web development, and the challenges you might face along the way.

You'll learn about the building blocks of web development, front-end developer tools, back-end programming languages, building jaw-dropping projects, making a stunning portfolio, and more.

[START AN ONLINE BUSINESS] Succeed Online When You're Young & My Experiences - Episode #1

This episode is all about how to start an online business, how access to the internet is an opportunity to learn valuable skills, and how having valuable skills (web development & content marketing) leads to financial independence.
