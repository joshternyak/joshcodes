We want you to write for the Josh Ternyak Blog

If you have knowledge, experience, or compelling stories on web development, productivity, or growing an online business, we want your words.

The Josh Ternyak Blog exists to help web developers, entrepreneurs, and online business geeks do 3 things:

  1. Learn Web Development
  2. Be productive
  3. Grow their online business

If you think you can help, we'd love to work with you.

How To Submit

This is a very easy-peasy process.

Here's how you submit a guest post:

  1. Read the rest of this page.
  2. Submit using the form below including a link to your Google Doc. We only review Google Docs.

Questions To Ask Yourself

  1. Who's your audience?
  2. What will readers get our of your article?
  3. Why should they read it?
  4. How will readers act on your article?
  5. Is your article valuable and unique?

Our Writing Style

Write in a casual, personal, and knowledgeable style. Write as if you were talking to your friend who has a passion of web development and growing an online business.

Aim for writing valuable content above wit, though you will get bonus points for doing both. Use images, lists, subheads, short sentences, lists, and other structural devices frequently.

You must have the right to use any image you include, and each image should have an alt tag describing what the image is showing. Make sure every image you include actually supports what you're writing, and isn't just there for making your post look nice.

You'll Get Full Credit

When you write a post for us, we'll not only add you as an author to our blog, you'll also get full credit and a high-quality backlink to your website or project you're currently working on.
