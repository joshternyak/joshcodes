My Ultimate Reading List: The Best Books I've Read

Here is a HUGE list of the books I think you should read. These books have changed my life, taught me valuable lessons, and helped me become a better person.

They've motivated me and taught me valuable skills that are not taught in school.

NOTE: I may earn money from links on this page, but I only recommend books I know will change your life.

I add on to this reading list every time I finish another GREAT book.

Let's get started.

The Slight Edge: Turning Simple Disciplines into Massive Success and Happiness

Rating: 10/10

The Slight Edge is a way of thinking that allows you to turn simple disciplines into massive success and happiness. Jeff Olson illustrates and explains what it takes to make your dreams come true.

It's the everyday habits and activities of your life that truly matter. You'll learn why some people make dream after dream come true, while others just keep dreaming and never get anywhere closer to the life they want.

This is a great self-help book to read first, because it will teach you that having good habits is what will lead you to massive success and happiness.

Outliers: The Story of Success

Rating: 9.7/10

Malcolm Gladwell takes readers on an intellectual journey through the world of "outliers". An outlier is someone who is the best, smartest, toughest, brightest, and most successful.

This book is thought-provoking and is very valuable for any person on earth. Filled with interesting stories followed up with lessons, you'll learn how to be an "outlier" if you read this book.

You'll read about the Roseto Mystery, the Matthew Effect, the 10,000 hour rule, and more. This book is explosively entertaining filled with compelling science, self-help, and entertainment, all in one book.

Crucial Conversations: Tools For Talking When Stakes Are High

Rating: 9.3/10

Crucial conversations are the conversations we have everyday. Whether you're talking to your boss, or even when you're trying to settle an agreement with your sibling, crucial conversations are happening all the time.

Kerry Patterson (Author) and Joseph Grenny (Narrator) give practical advice you can start using today. They'll teach you how to:

  1. Prepare for high-stake conversations
  2. Make it safe to talk about almost anything
  3. Transform unpleasant emotions into powerful and peaceful dialogue
  4. Be persuasive and not be abrasive

So, if you want to achieve relationships that are productive, real, and that will enrich your life and career, this book is a must-read.

If you want your business to grow and generate more traffic and conversions, then DotComSecrets is the book you should read. Russell Brunson is someone who has built one of the most successful businesses teaching entrepreneurs how to grow their businesses that lead to exceptional results.

Sleep: The Myth of 8 Hours, the Power of Naps, and the New Plan to Recharge Your Body and Mind

Rating: 9.5/10
