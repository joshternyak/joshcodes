About Me

I've been telling computers what to do since the age of 11, when my brother introduced me to python.

Once you learn design, you feel fine.

I began my coding journey at age 11 by learning Python day and night. After a year of learning, I was introduced to front-end web development. As soon as I made my first website, my brain exploded with creativity. I spent the next year learning how to make a website look nice.

After kicking off my journey with tons of tutorials and stress, I started building websites for clients. This changed the way I thought about web development and the challenges that come with it.

In between client work, I found time to learn UI design and how to keep your code clean. I learned how important it is to not only make stunning websites, but keep the code clean and structured.

Soon after, I worked on projects with my brother which led to me having a solid understanding of css, UI design, and project management. I am currently working on building website themes that make a difference.
